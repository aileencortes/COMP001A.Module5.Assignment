Ways To Use Them

Corn Flakes Fried Chicken

Here's an example of how you can use Corn Flakes as a breading for your chicken. When seasoned properly you wont even taste the fact that it's Corn Flakes cereal. It also provides a crunchy texture to make your fry chicken extra crunchy.

Raisin Bran Oatmeal Cookie

In this vide you will get a tutorial on how to use Raisin Bran to make a simple Raisin and Oatmeal Cookie. Raisin Bran helps provides the raisins needed for the cookie and an amazing cookie texture.

Rice Krispies Treats

You can always buy Rice Krispies Treats at the store or you could choice to save some coin and make them at home. You could also choose to use the Rice Krispies Chocolate cereal and make them chocolate flavored.
